Our Restaurant

Welcome to Santos Restaurant, located in downtown Naugatuck on 152 Church Street, here at Santos whether you are here for the first time the moment you walk in you will feel the family atmospehere so whether you stop by for a drink or to taste our delicious Portuguese cuisine we are glad that you are here! We not only have exceptional food and drinks, we are also able to provide a great dining experience for you and your family, banquet, weddings, and other events, we are here to help.
